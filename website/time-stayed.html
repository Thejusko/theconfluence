People over time stayed

This graph represents something simple: How many people stayed how many weeks. Of course, the great majority of them stayed only one week, because they did not participate at all. But one important thing to note is that the amount people that stayed two weeks has a similar characteristic, given that on some weeks there was no run, meaning that users that did not participate at all got a free extra week for nothing.
Because we can't really see what's going on with the first graph, the second one shows only people that stayed 3 weeks minimum.
